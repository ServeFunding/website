## SEO/AI Best Practices

✅ **DO:**
- Place video near top of section (above the fold)
- Include full transcript for searchability
- Use question phrasing people actually ask
- Add VideoObject schema
- Keep videos 2-4 minutes
- Update content within 30 days (3.2x more AI citations)
- Build answer capsule before video (standalone summary)

❌ **DON'T:**
- Bury videos deep on page
- Skip transcripts (loses SEO value)
- Use generic phrases ("Learn about" vs. "What is")
- Make videos longer than 5 minutes
- Forget to update regularly
- Use videos without supporting text
