### C. FEE / PRICING TRANSPARENCY

> [16:04](https://fathom.video/calls/567877438?timestamp=964) Sterling Birdsong: "Here's the issue with that, Michael, is that if the bank knows that you're paying, that the customer's already paying a success fee, then they won't pay a success fee themselves, right? **The packaging fee workaround — hourly invoice that backs into the allowable amount:**
> [16:04](https://fathom.video/calls/567877438?timestamp=964) Sterling Birdsong: "That's why that, that little thing, that packaging fee is not technically a success fee. It is a packaging fee for the work that I've done to get the file... And so I actually have to provide an hourly invoice for that packaging fee. Now, what everybody in the industry kind of knows is that, that. That hourly invoice usually equates to exactly what you're able to charge, right? Which is the 2% on the first million, and then 25 basis points on every million after that. So most people kind of back into what that number is based on the loan amount, and then they're basically charging that, they're breaking that down to an hourly rate, okay? That's how they're basically working to make it SBA eligible..."

**How banks cap their own packaging fee vs. the broker's:**
